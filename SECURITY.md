# Security Policy

Please do not disclose vulnerabilities in a public issue. Report them privately
to `opensource@limxdynamics.com`. GitHub private vulnerability reporting may
also be used when it is enabled for this repository.

Include the affected revision, reproduction steps, impact, and suggested
mitigation. Do not send active credentials; revoke or rotate them through the
appropriate provider and describe the exposure without including the secret.

Do not include credentials, private robot data, or other confidential material.
Issues in `limxsdk-lowlevel` or `robot-description` should also be reported to
their respective maintainers.
